## Queue — Priority A (next 8 weeks)

**04. Causes of Death, Year by Year** 📈
The leading cause of death for every year of age from 1 to 100. Reveals that each decade has a different primary enemy. Data source: CDC WONDER, WHO mortality data.

**05. The Shrinking Chocolate Bar** 📉
Cadbury, Mars, Snickers, Toblerone weights since 1970. Inflation-adjusted price per gram. Shrinkflation as a behavioural insight.

**06. Where the Billionaires Actually Sleep** 🗺️
Forbes 500 primary residences vs registered HQs. The map of real residence is 9 cities plus a few islands. Data: Forbes, public filings.

**07. The Global Tipping Atlas** 🗺️
Expected tip % by country and service (restaurant, taxi, hotel, bar). Three-camp model: obligation, included, insult.

**08. Names That Died** 📉
Top 10 US baby names of 1920 that no longer appear. Overlay with names that went 0-to-top-10 in one decade. Data: SSA baby name data.

**09. How the World Wakes Up** 🗺️
Average phone-first-unlock time by country. Source: aggregated phone data studies, academic research on chronotypes.

**10. The Michelin vs McDonald's Map** 🗺️
Density of each per capita by country. Cultural story via two dots per city.

**11. The Loneliness Curve** 📈
Single-person households by country from 1960 to now. Japan, Sweden, US trajectories.

**12. One Day in the Life of the Median Human** 🔍
Stacked 24-hour chart: sleep, work, eat, commute, phone, sex, leisure. Global average with outlier countries annotated. Source: OECD time-use surveys.

## Queue — Priority B (next 6 months)

**13. The Global Wedding Cost Atlas** 🗺️
Average wedding cost as % of annual income by country. India, Nigeria, US at the top.

**14. Ages of "Adulthood"** ▦
Drinking age, driving age, voting age, age of consent, marriage age, criminal responsibility age across 50 countries. Small multiples.

**15. The Shrinking Airline Seat** 📉
Seat pitch in economy class over 60 years. Data from SeatGuru archives and airline disclosures.

**16. Divorce Rates by Marriage Longevity** 📈
Probability of divorce at each year of marriage. Reveals the 7-year-itch myth vs reality.

**17. Where the Migrants Go** 🗺️
Net migration flows for every country, visualised as weighted chords. Data: UN DESA.

**18. Military Spending Per Capita** 🗺️
Per-capita defence spending by country. Reveals that small rich countries often spend more per head than big powers.

**19. The Attention Timeline** 📉
Average movie shot length, pop song intro length, TikTok video length, tweet length since 1980. The acceleration story.

**20. Reading Speed Over a Lifetime** 📈
Words-per-minute reading speed from age 6 to 85. Peaks mid-30s, plateaus, declines.

**21. The Global Coffee-vs-Tea Map** 🗺️
Per-capita consumption of each. Clear cultural-historical boundary lines.

**22. What the Rich Leave in Their Wills** ▦
Categorical breakdown of HNW inheritance patterns across countries. Data: Knight Frank Wealth Report.

**23. The "First Phone" Age Trend** 📉
Average age of first smartphone ownership, year by year. Data: Pew, OFCOM.

**24. Where the World's Prisons Are** 🗺️
Incarceration rate per 100k by country. US leads by a wide margin.

- **Add ideas freely.** Any piece you'd like to exist, add here. The queue is meant to grow.
- **When an idea ships, move it to `research/completed/`** as an archived record.
