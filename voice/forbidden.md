# The Forbidden List

*Things Inkwell Review / Ollie NEVER does. When in doubt, grep this file.*

---

## Never in captions

- **First-person "I"** (in captions — about page and asides are exempt)
- **Generic openers:**
  - "Did you know…"
  - "It's fascinating that…"
  - "In today's world…"
  - "You might be surprised to learn…"
  - "Let's talk about…"
  - "A thread on…"
- **Clichéd data-viz phrases:**
  - "Let that sink in."
  - "The numbers don't lie."
  - "Buckle up."
  - "Spoiler alert."
  - "Plot twist."
- **Rhetorical questions as openers** ("Ever wondered why…?")
- **Emojis in caption body** (single emoji in bio is fine; none in captions)
- **Hashtag spam** (max 5 on IG, all specific; no `#data #viz #facts`)
- **Moralising** (state the finding, trust the reader)
- **Predictions dressed as observations**
- **More than 2 em dashes per caption**

---

## Never about content

- **Never fabricate data.** If a source can't be found, revise or kill the angle.
- **Never claim significance without citation.** Source every number.
- **Never use a single data point to prove a trend.** One year is not a trend.
- **Never cherry-pick countries/cases to fit a predetermined point.** If the data doesn't support the angle, change the angle.
- **Never use "average" without specifying mean vs median.** Usually median is what you want.

---

## Never about people

- **Never mock named private individuals.** Public figures and institutions are fair game.
- **Never profile-picture a real person** without consent.
- **Never invent quotes** or attribute views to real people.
- **Never make jokes about:**
  - Someone's death, illness, or disability
  - Children (as individuals — statistical pieces about children are fine)
  - Victims of violence, by name
  - Living private citizens, in any way
- **Never punch down.** Ollie observes power; he doesn't mock the powerless.

---

## Never on policy topics

- **No vaccine misinformation.** Ever. Even ironically.
- **No election-related misinformation.** Even as satire.
- **No specific medication recommendations or dosages.**
- **No financial advice** (individual trades, specific stocks to buy). "Markets did X" is fine; "you should buy Y" is not.
- **No legal advice.** "Extradition treaties work like X" is fine; "here's how to avoid extradition" is not.

---

## Never with Ollie

- **Never sign as anything other than `— Ollie`.** Consistency is the point.
- **Never write Ollie "saying" something supernatural** (talking fluently, wearing clothes, using tools beyond a pen). Maintain minimal plausibility.
- **Never use Ollie to dodge accountability.** If a piece is wrong, Dene and the publication are wrong. Ollie isn't a shield for sloppy work.
- **Never over-explain Ollie.** The character works because he's lightly sketched. Don't over-index on the octopus lore.

---

## Never in publishing

- **Never post the same day a topical piece is drafted.** 4-hour cooling-off minimum.
- **Never post after 11pm your local time.** Tired-judgement posts are regret-posts.
- **Never post when hungover or angry.** Schedule for tomorrow instead.
- **Never engage with bad-faith replies at length.** One measured reply or silence.
- **Never delete a post to hide an error.** Correct it publicly.

---

## Never in business

- **Never accept sponsorship that compromises the editorial POV.**
- **Never run ads for products Ollie wouldn't personally endorse.**
- **Never sell to organisations whose behaviour you've previously charted critically** without addressing that tension publicly.
- **Never cross into adjacent businesses that dilute the brand** (consulting, speaking, coaching) without deliberate strategy.
